PRINTER NOTES

1. Max length of a line is 48 characters
2. the \ character gets treated as newline or \n
3. You can print loooooooong stuff
4. Cutting is an (hardware) issue
5. Blanks notes can be made. (maybe to start it up or inbetween messages?)
6. Images can be printed if they are in JPEG format
